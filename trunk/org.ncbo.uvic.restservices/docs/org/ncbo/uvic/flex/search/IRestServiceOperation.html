Packageorg.ncbo.uvic.flex.search
Interfacepublic interface IRestServiceOperation extends flash.events.IEventDispatcher

Performs a search operation on the rest service. This might include getting the children of a concept, getting the parents of a concept, or a combination of the two. These operations can also be combined to form multiple operations performed in sequence or in parallel.
